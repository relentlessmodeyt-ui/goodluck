About Aaruni

A hospital with roots in this community

Founded by eminent orthopaedist Dr. Sunil Poonia (MBBS, MS Orthopedics), Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital is Rajasthan's fastest-growing NABH-accredited institution — dedicated to bringing advanced, compassionate care to every family in Jhunjhunu and beyond.

  • Advanced ICU with BiPAP, ventilator & round-the-clock monitoring
  • In-house MRI, CT, USG, Echo, X-Ray & full pathology lab
  • Modular OTs, NICU, Laparoscopy & Reconstructive Surgery
  • Surgical Gastroenterology, Nephrology & Vascular Surgery
  • 24×7 Trauma centre, Ambulance & on-site Pharmacy
