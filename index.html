Our Story

Built on trust,
driven by care

Founded in 2020 by Dr. Sunil Poonia (MBBS, MS Orthopedics), Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital was born from a single belief — that the people of Jhunjhunu deserve world-class medical care without travelling to distant cities. In just four years, we've treated over 15,000 patients and earned full NABH accreditation, the gold standard in Indian healthcare quality.

We combine the latest technology with warm, personal care. From emergency trauma to elective joint replacements, from high-risk deliveries to complex neurological cases — Aaruni stands by Jhunjhunu, always.

    Modular OTs with laminar airflow, advanced anaesthesia workstations and complete laparoscopic tower for minimally invasive surgery.

  5. 05
