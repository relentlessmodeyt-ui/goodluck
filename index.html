About Aaruni

A multispeciality hospital built around the patient

Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital is among the fastest-growing healthcare institutions in Rajasthan, delivering top-quality, patient-focused care to the people of Jhunjhunu and the surrounding region. From everyday consultations to complex surgeries, we combine clinical excellence with genuine compassion.

Our NABH-accredited facility is equipped with advanced diagnostics, modern operation theatres and a 24×7 intensive care unit — staffed by experienced specialists committed to safe, personalised treatment.

  • Highly skilled, multi-disciplinary medical team
  • State-of-the-art diagnostic & surgical technology
  • Round-the-clock ICU, trauma & ambulance support
  • Transparent, patient-centric approach to care
