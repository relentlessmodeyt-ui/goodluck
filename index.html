01 Who we are

We are a team of eminent doctors and care-givers obsessed with one outcome — getting you back to life, faster and stronger.

Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital was founded in 2020 to bring tertiary-level healthcare to Jhunjhunu and the wider Shekhawati region. Today we are NABH accredited, combining state-of-the-art infrastructure with a genuinely human approach to medicine.

From planned surgeries to the unplanned emergency at 3 AM, our teams move with precision and compassion — because in medicine, motion saves lives.
