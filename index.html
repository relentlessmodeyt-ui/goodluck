About Aaruni

A hospital built around the patient

Founded by eminent orthopaedist Dr. Sunil Poonia (MBBS, MS Orthopedics), Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital is among the fastest-growing NABH-accredited institutions in Rajasthan — delivering advanced, compassionate care to Jhunjhunu and beyond since 2020.

  • Highly skilled, multi-disciplinary specialist team
  • Advanced ICU with BiPAP, ventilator & continuous monitoring
  • In-house MRI, CT, USG, X-Ray, Echo & Pathology lab
  • Modular OTs, NICU, Laparoscopy & Reconstructive Surgery
  • Surgical Gastroenterology, Nephrology & Vascular Surgery
  • 24×7 Trauma, Ambulance & On-site Pharmacy

Quick answers to the questions on every visitor's mind before they walk through our doors.
