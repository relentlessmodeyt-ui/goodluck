Why Apex

Families in Jhunjhunu trust us with what matters most

Established in 2021, Apex Skyline Hospital is a 70-bed multi-speciality hospital in the heart of Jhunjhunu — built around advanced cardiac care, with a 24/7 Cardiac ICU, a cath lab and onsite cardiologists, alongside orthopaedics, neurology and critical care. NABH & NABL accredited, with a genuinely human approach — whether it's a planned procedure or an emergency at 3 AM.

  • NABH & NABL accredited quality & patient safety
  • 24/7 Cardiac ICU with onsite cardiologists
  • Cardiac cath lab & non-invasive labs (Echo · TMT · Holter)
  • Multi-speciality care: cardiology, orthopaedics, neurology & critical care

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

What is Apex Hospitals and where is it located?

A NABH-accredited chain of super-speciality hospitals with 30+ years of experience and 850+ beds — across Jaipur (Malviya Nagar & Mansarovar), Sawai Madhopur, Bikaner, Jhunjhunu, Sri Ganganagar and Udaipur.

What medical services and specialities does Apex offer?

Full super-speciality care — cardiology & cardiovascular surgery, robotic surgery, ICU & critical care, oncology, urology, orthopaedics and many more — all under one roof.

Is Apex Hospitals accredited for quality & safety?

Yes. Apex is NABH-accredited (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers), assuring high standards of patient care and safe hospital processes.

How experienced is the medical team?

200+ senior doctors across specialities and super-specialities — consultants, surgeons and critical-care experts — backed by skilled nurses and technicians for coordinated, high-quality care.

Does Apex provide emergency & critical care?

Yes — 24/7 emergency and trauma care, with advanced ICUs and critical-care support for urgent, life-threatening conditions.

Can I book appointments or consult doctors?

Yes — book appointments, schedule video consultations and view lab reports through the Apex app or website.
