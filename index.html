Our Story

Rooted in Jhunjhunu,
driven by care

Founded in 2020 by Dr. Sunil Poonia (MBBS, MS Orthopedics), Aaruni Multispeciality Hospital was born from a singular vision: to bring specialist-level healthcare to the people of Jhunjhunu without requiring them to travel to distant cities.

Over five years, we have grown into a trusted multispeciality institution — NABH accredited, staffed by experienced specialists, equipped with modern diagnostics, and driven by the belief that every patient deserves the highest standard of care close to home.
